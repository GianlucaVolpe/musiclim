How it works

Three steps to owning
a track no one else can copy

01

Browse the catalog

Find original tracks released in limited editions. Each listing shows how many copies remain and the price set by the artist — no auctions, no speculation.

02

Buy your copy

Pay securely via Stripe. Your copy is immediately registered on the ownership ledger with a unique audio watermark embedded in the file — no one else can claim it.

03

Listen or resell

Stream your tracks in full quality from any device, encrypted end-to-end. Or list your copy on the secondary market at a price you choose — peer-to-peer, no intermediaries.
